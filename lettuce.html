Description:

The tomato is the edible berry of the plant Solanum lycopersicum, commonly known as a tomato plant.

Where does it grow?

Tomatoes are native to South America. Tomato plants thrive in full sun and grow in rich, well-drained soil.

Fun facts!

Tomato plants are one of the few vegetables that will root along the stem
